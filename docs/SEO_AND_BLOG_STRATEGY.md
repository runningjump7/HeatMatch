## Blog Strategy (To Start Later)

### Publishing Cadence
**Target:** 1 article per week, published consistently (e.g., every Tuesday)

**Why weekly:** Enough frequency to signal freshness to Google and build topical authority without overwhelming your schedule. Monthly is too sparse; daily is unsustainable at this stage.

### Content Pillars & Topic Ideas

**Pillar 1: Education (60% of posts)**
Help homeowners understand heat pumps—build trust, not just rank.

Examples:
- "How Much Does a Heat Pump Installation Cost in Auckland?" (Answer: $X-Y, factors, rebates)
- "Heat Pumps vs Traditional Heating: Which is Right for Your Home?"
- "5 Signs Your Heat Pump Needs Service"
- "What's the Best Heat Pump Brand for New Zealand?"
- "Heat Pump Installation Process: A Step-by-Step Guide"

**Pillar 2: Local (20% of posts)**
Target suburb-specific keywords and build neighborhood presence.

Examples:
- "Heat Pump Installers in Albany: What to Expect"
- "Why North Shore Homes Are Perfect for Heat Pumps"
- "Takapuna Heat Pump Guide: Local Rebates & Tips"

**Pillar 3: Seasonal (20% of posts)**
Tie to NZ seasons and pain points.

Examples:
- "Summer Heat Pump Tips: Staying Cool Efficiently" (Oct-Dec)
- "Winter Installation Checklist: Get Ready Before the Rush" (May-Jun)
- "Spring HVAC Maintenance for Heat Pumps" (Sep-Oct)

### SEO Execution

Each post should:
1. **Target one primary keyword** (e.g., "heat pump installation cost Auckland")
2. **Include suburb name if local** (increases relevance for local search)
3. **Answer user questions** (H2 subheadings = common questions)
4. **Internal link to relevant pages** (e.g., link "Get a Free Quote" CTA)
5. **Use simple, clear language** (not keyword-stuffed; readability matters for Google and users)
6. **800-1200 words** (long enough to rank, short enough to write weekly)

### Tone & Voice
- **For homeowners:** Friendly, helpful, non-salesy. You're a guide, not a pushy salesperson.
- **Example:** "Heat pumps can seem intimidating, but the installation process is straightforward..." (vs. "Our installers are the best!")
- **Avoid:** Excessive jargon, marketing fluff, competitor bashing.

### Content Calendar Template
```
Week 1: Education - "Heat Pump Basics"
Week 2: Local - "[Suburb] Guide"
Week 3: Seasonal / Trending
Week 4: Education - FAQ Response
(Repeat)
```

### Tools to Use
- **Keyword research:** Ubersuggest, Ahrefs (free tier), or Google Search Console
- **SEO checker:** Yoast or Surfer SEO (free versions available)
- **Internal linking:** Link recent posts to old ones and to landing pages

### Expected Timeline
- **Month 1-2:** Establish rhythm, build topical authority (8-10 posts)
- **Month 3+:** Should start seeing search traffic trickle in
- **Month 6+:** Meaningful organic traffic from blogs + suburb pages + GBP
