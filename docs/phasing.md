# TRADEEV2 — Phasing Plan (MVP → MVP+1 → MVP+2)

## MVP: Directory + Lead Capture (Weeks 1–3)

**Goal:** Validate that (1) heat pump installers will pay for leads, (2) customers will book through the platform.

### Customer-Facing
- Homepage with value prop & suburb filter
- Installer directory (list + detail pages)
- Lead capture form (name, email, phone, suburb, description)
- SEO landing pages (`/takapuna-heat-pump-installer`, etc.)

### Installer-Facing (Authenticated)
- Installer login (Clerk)
- Dashboard: view leads, edit profile, simple analytics
- Analytics: weekly searches, profile views, call/email clicks, leads received

### Admin Panel
- Installer approval workflow (verify EWRB + EPA Refrigerant License)
- Platform metrics dashboard

**Success metrics (Week 3):**
- 15–20 approved installers
- 50+ customer leads captured
- >50% leads marked "interested" by installers
- 30%+ ready to pay subscription

---

## MVP+1: Subscriptions + Reviews (Weeks 4–8)

**Goal:** Monetize with subscriptions, improve lead quality.

### New Features
- Subscription plans: Starter ($99/mo), Pro ($299/mo)
- Premium lead routing (Pro → Starter)
- Customer review system (5-star + comment)
- Advanced analytics per installer
- Review moderation + "Top-rated" badges

**Success metrics (Week 8):**
- MRR ≥$3k
- 30%+ installer upgrade rate
- <10% monthly churn
- 20+ reviews (avg 4+ stars)

---

## MVP+2: Geographic Expansion + AI (Weeks 9–16)

**Goal:** Scale to 3+ Auckland suburbs, add AI lead matching.

### New Features
- Expand to West Auckland, Central Auckland
- AI diagnostic assistant (issue triage → best installer match)
- Installer specialization tags
- Advanced installer filtering

**Success metrics (Week 16):**
- MRR ≥$8k
- 3 geographic markets
- 30+ reviews across all areas
- AI improves lead quality by 15%+
